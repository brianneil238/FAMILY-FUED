Game Overview

Family Feud is a game where two teams compete to guess the most popular answers to survey questions. The goal is to score the highest points by providing correct answers.

How to Play

  1. Two teams will take turns answering questions.
  2. Each question will have a set of popular answers based on surveys.
  3. The team that answers correctly gets points based on the popularity of their answer.
  4. If a team cannot answer, they can pass the turn to the other team.
  5. The game continues until all questions have been answered.

Scoring

Points are awarded based on the popularity of the answers:

  • 1 point for each answer revealed on the board.
  • The team with the highest points at the end of the game wins!

Special Rules

Each team has a limited time to answer, typically 60 seconds. If the time runs out, the question is passed to the other team.
